Our Story

Welcome to our website! We are a company dedicated to producing the highest quality peanut butter products for our customers. Our journey began many years ago, Since then, we have been committed to using only the finest ingredients in our peanut butter products, including high-quality peanuts sourced from trusted farmers, and natural flavors and sweeteners that are free from artificial preservatives and additives. We take great pride in our peanut butter products and are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible experience. Our team of experts carefully crafts each batch to ensure that it meets our high standards of quality and taste.

  • Mission By utilizing modern technologies to manufacture high-quality products that meet the needs of customers and promote human health, while also prioritizing an effective organizational and operational structure, we can establish ourselves as a preferred and competitive player in the food industry.
  • Vission To see the food industry as a competitive and preferred model in Ethiopia and Africa in 2034.
  • Values Sincerity,Loyalty,Accountability,Fairness,Working together,Guided by a plan,Professional ethics and respect for work, Caring for public resources,Supremacy of law.
